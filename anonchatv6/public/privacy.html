Privacy & Safety

This app stores some data to keep the community safe and improve reliability.

  • SQLite database (data/anonchat.db) keeps room settings and messages.
  • Audit entries: IP (or salted hash if SAVE_RAW_IP=0), user agent, approx geo via geoip-lite.
  • Uploads are written to /uploads (local disk).

Retention: Decide your own deletion policy. Consider hashing IPs by setting SAVE_RAW_IP=0 and IP_SALT.
